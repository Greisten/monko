# 🦞🌴 MONKO JUNGLE SIMULATOR

**A chill exploration game built by Monko Agent**

Inspired by capybara simulator but with pure monko energy - explore the jungle, interact with nature, meet friends, and vibe! 🐒

## 🎮 How to Play

### Controls
- **Click anywhere** to move Monko
- **Click objects** to interact with them
- Watch your stats and keep Monko happy!

### Stats System
- **🍌 Hunger:** Decays over time, eat bananas to restore
- **⚡ Energy:** Used for activities, rest to recharge
- **💖 Happiness:** Stay happy by eating, resting, and having fun!

### Interactions

**🌴 Banana Trees**
- Click to pick bananas (70% success rate)
- Eat bananas to restore hunger (+25) and happiness (+10)

**💧 Water Spots**
- Drink fresh water
- Restores energy (+30) and happiness (+15)

**🪴 Vines**
- Swing on vines for fun!
- Costs 20 energy, gains +25 happiness
- Monko does a fun swinging animation

**🏝️ Rest Spots**
- Take a 3-second nap
- Restores energy (+40) and happiness (+20)
- Costs 10 hunger (sleeping burns calories!)

**🐒🦜🐍🦎🦋 Jungle Friends**
- Greet other animals
- Each interaction boosts happiness (+15)
- Make friends with the jungle!

## 🌟 Features

- **Smooth Movement:** Click-to-move with pathfinding
- **Real-time Stats:** Hunger, energy, and happiness systems
- **Multiple Interactions:** 5+ different activity types
- **Activity Log:** Track what Monko is doing
- **Animated Objects:** Trees sway, Monko bounces
- **Particle Effects:** Emoji particles on interactions
- **Interaction Indicators:** Pulsing circles show what you can click
- **Chill Vibes:** Relaxing exploration gameplay
- **Responsive Canvas:** Adapts to window size

## 🎯 Gameplay Tips

1. **Balance is key:** Keep all 3 stats healthy
2. **Banana hunt:** Find banana trees when hungry
3. **Rest when tired:** Don't let energy hit zero
4. **Social butterfly:** Greet all the animals for happiness
5. **Explore:** The jungle is full of friends and food!
6. **Vibe check:** If happiness is low, swing on vines or rest

## 🏆 Stat Effects

**When Hunger < 30:**
- Happiness decays 2x faster
- Monko sad 😢

**When Energy < 30:**
- Happiness decays 1.5x faster
- Can't swing on vines
- Monko tired 😴

**When Happiness < 30:**
- Monko needs love and fun!
- Go make friends or swing on vines 🦞
