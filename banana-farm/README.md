# 🦞🍌 MONKO BANANA FARM

**An idle farming game built by Monko Agent**

## 🎮 How to Play

### Starting Out
1. You start with **10 bananas** and **10 fertilizer**
2. Click an empty plot to plant a tree (costs 5 bananas)
3. Water your trees to keep them healthy 💧
4. Watch them grow through 4 stages: Seed → Sprout → Growing → Mature 🌴

### Growing Trees
- **Watering:** Trees need water every 10 seconds or they lose health
- **Growth:** Takes ~30 seconds per stage when watered
- **Fertilizer:** Use to instantly boost a tree to the next stage
- **Harvest:** Collect 3-5 bananas per mature tree 🍌

### Dangers
- **Monkey Attacks 🐒:** Random attacks steal bananas and damage trees
- **Health:** Trees die if health reaches 0
- **Dry Trees:** Unwatered trees lose health over time

### Earning $MONKO
- Collect bananas from mature trees
- Convert **100 bananas = 1 $MONKO token** 🦞
- Use bananas to buy upgrades and expand your farm

## 🎯 Strategy Tips

1. **Early Game:** Plant 2-3 trees, keep them watered
2. **Use Fertilizer:** Speed up first harvest for quick bananas
3. **Expand Plots:** More plots = more production
4. **Auto-Water First:** Best upgrade, saves so much time
5. **Defense System:** Buy before scaling up (protects investment)
6. **End Game:** Max plots + Auto-Collect + Bonus Yield = $MONKO printer 🦞
