## 🎮 GAME 1: MONKO BANANA FARM

**Genre:** Idle / Farming Simulation  
**Play:** [Open Banana Farm](./banana-farm/index.html)

### 🌴 Features
- **Plant & Grow:** Watch banana trees grow through 4 stages (Seed → Sprout → Growing → Mature)
- **Water & Fertilize:** Keep your trees healthy with water (💧) and boost growth with fertilizer (🌱)
- **Harvest Bananas:** Collect 3-5 bananas per mature tree 🍌
- **Defend Your Farm:** Random monkey attacks (🐒) steal bananas - buy defense upgrades!
- **Earn $MONKO:** Convert 100 bananas → 1 $MONKO token 🦞
- **6 Shop Upgrades:**
  - New Plot (100🍌) - Add 4 more plots
  - Auto-Water (500🍌) - Trees water themselves
  - Auto-Collect (750🍌) - Bananas collect automatically
  - Monkey Defense (1000🍌) - 90% attack protection
  - Fast Grow (2000🍌) - 2x growth speed
  - Bonus Yield (3000🍌) - +50% bananas per tree
- **Auto-Save:** Your progress saves automatically

### 🎯 How to Play
1. Start with 10 bananas - plant your first tree (costs 5 bananas)
2. Water trees regularly or they lose health
3. Use fertilizer to instantly boost tree growth
4. Harvest mature trees for bananas
5. Defend against monkey attacks
6. Convert bananas to $MONKO tokens
7. Buy upgrades to automate and scale your farm

**Goal:** Build a massive banana empire and stack $MONKO tokens! From exile to zillions through farming! 🌴→💎

## 🎮 GAME 2: MONKO JUNGLE SIMULATOR

**Genre:** Chill Exploration / Life Sim  
**Play:** [Open Jungle Simulator](./jungle-simulator/index.html)

### 🌴 Features
- **Click to Move:** Explore the jungle freely
- **3 Vital Stats:**
  - 🍌 **Hunger** - Decays over time, eat bananas to restore
  - ⚡ **Energy** - Used for activities, rest to recharge
  - 💖 **Happiness** - Stay happy through eating, resting, and fun!
- **Interactive World:**
  - 🌴 **Banana Trees** - Pick bananas (70% success rate)
  - 💧 **Water Spots** - Drink to restore energy
  - 🪴 **Vines** - Swing for fun! (costs energy, gains happiness)
  - 🏝️ **Rest Spots** - Take naps to recharge
  - 🐒🦜🐍🦎🦋 **Jungle Friends** - Greet animals to boost happiness
- **Activity Log:** Track everything Monko does
- **Real-time Stat Decay:** Manage hunger, energy, and happiness
- **Smooth Animations:** Swaying trees, bouncing Monko, particle effects
- **Interaction Indicators:** Pulsing circles show what you can click

### 🎯 How to Play
1. Click anywhere to move Monko
2. Click on objects when nearby to interact
3. Keep all 3 stats balanced for maximum vibes
4. Explore the jungle, collect bananas, meet friends
5. Rest when tired, eat when hungry, have fun when sad

**Goal:** Just vibe! Pure capybara-style chill exploration. No pressure, just monko energy in the jungle. 🦞🌴
